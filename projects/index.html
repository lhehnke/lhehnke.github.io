Accident analyses

Aviation accidents (replication data; script for projecting, mapping, and analyzing crashes in Florida 2014 with point pattern analyses) [slightly outdated]

Road accidents (script for geocoding, mapping, and visually analyzing crashes in South Australia 2016)

Death penalties (script for scraping, wrangling, mapping, and visualizing historical data on executions in the United States, 1800-1900)

Serial killers (data; scripts for scraping, geocoding, visually analyzing, and mapping Wikipedia data on international and US American serial killers)
