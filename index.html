Hello, Reader!

I'm Lisa and I'm a social data scientist working to address current societal challenges in order to make a difference and drive change. I also volunteer at CorrelAid, a network of data scientists who use their skills to support nonprofit organizations. Other than that, I'm passionate about aviation, psychology, and playing around with data on fun topics, such as song lyrics or serial killers.

In computing, data planes are the parts of a network that carry user traffic. Data Planes builds on this idea by carrying information on all things data and sharing reproducible code for my own projects, aiming to contribute to both the scientific community and the Open Science initiative.
