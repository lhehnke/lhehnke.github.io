Hello, Reader!

I'm Lisa and I'm a computational social scientist working to address societal challenges in an interdisciplinary manner and freelance data scientist. I also am a core team member of CorrelAid, a volunteer network of data scientists who apply their skills for the social good by supporting nonprofit organizations. Other than that, I'm passionate about aviation, psychology, and playing around with data on fun topics, such as song lyrics or serial killers.

In computing, data planes are the parts of a network that carry user traffic. Data Planes builds on this idea by carrying information on all things data and sharing reproducible code for my own projects, aiming to contribute to both the scientific community and the Open Science initiative.
