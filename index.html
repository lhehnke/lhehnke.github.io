Hello, Reader!

I'm Lisa and I dedicate most of my available time to making a difference. In my day-to-day life I democratize the way we collaboratively work together with Liberate Science, use data for good at CorrelAid, and am involved with the Open Science MOOC to openly share scientific knowledge. Other than that, I'm strangely curious about the mechanisms behind aviation accidents, value continuous learning and multipotentiality, and advocate for ethical leadership and gender equity.

As a freelance social data scientist I support organizations in harnessing the potential of their data to improve strategic decision-making and maximize their impact. In addition, I regularly give paid and pro bono talks and workshops on data-related topics as I strongly believe that it is the people rather than the data who drive positive change.
