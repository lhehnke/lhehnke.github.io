Hello, Reader!

I'm Lisa and I'm a researcher at the University of Bielefeld, where I mainly focus on studying and teaching (computational) social research methods. Other than that, I'm passionate about aviation, psychology, and playing around with data on fun topics, such as song lyrics or serial killers.

In computing, data planes are the parts of a network that carry user traffic. Data Planes builds on this idea by carrying information on data science and sharing reproducible code for my own projects, aiming to contribute to both the programming community and the Open Science initiative.
